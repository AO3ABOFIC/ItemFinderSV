Welcome Treasured Friends!

Have you been experiencing difficulties locating items to assist you in your Treasure Hunt? The world is vast and there’s no shame in needing a little help during your adventure. For the convenience of all participating in this year’s annual Treasure Hunt, here is a handy, easy to navigate database on any and all items that could help you over the course of your Treasure Hunt!

We recommend first checking the About tab if this is your first time using our application, also, for people participating in the Treasure Hunt for the first time this year, the Information tab contains plenty of useful information regarding terminology that may be new to you on your Treasure Hunt and how to fully understand all of the information provided to you with this application.

Now, may your Treasure Hunt be a fruitful experience! Onward!
