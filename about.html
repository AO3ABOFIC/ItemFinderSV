About This Application

This application was designed as the product for an IB Computer Science IA and for the video games Pokemon Scarlet and Violet. The product is an interactive database providing the spawn data of every item in the game including location, spawn rate, and a built in calculator to identify and provide users with the exact percentage chance of receiving multiples of an item at once.

The product was coded utilizing mainly HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and is deployed and accessible with Github Pages.

For those unfamiliar with the franchise or video games in general I recommend visiting the pages under the Information tab as it will help define some of the terminology used throughout the application as well as how presented information such as calculations are formulated and how to properly read the percentages provided by the application in the context of the game.
