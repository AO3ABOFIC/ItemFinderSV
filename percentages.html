Percentages

The percentages presented within the application follow a specific format to best represent the in game circumstances they represent, examples and explanations can be found bellow.

For the majority of items, the initial percentage provided will be the exact percent chance that a randomly generated shining item spot will produce one of a specific item capable of spawning in the area.

For static item spawns the percentage will be automatically set to 100% with the limited quantity listed.

When calculating for the percent chance of receiving multiple of an item from a shining item spot the percentage must fall within the valid limits of the acquisition circumstances such as:

Items that can only be found one at a time will never surpass their singular spawn percentage

Items that can be received in bulk may have a maximum limit which the quantity must never exceed for calculations.
