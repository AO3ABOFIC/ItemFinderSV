Item Finder SV

Percentages

The quantity presented within the application follow a specific format to best represent the in game circumstances they represent, examples and explanations can be found bellow.

For the majority of items, the initial quantity provided will be the exact number of the given item you can receive at that specific location in the given area.

Please note:

Items with a presented numerical quantity can not respawn or be obtained more than once unless specified

Items without a presented numerical quantity can respawn in or around their original location in the given area, items respawn at random time intervals typically through a shining spot on the ground and the item is a random item from the selection of respawning items in the area.
